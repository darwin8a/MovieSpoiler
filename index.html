Plot

"When one of Harlem's leading mobster's dies, his chauffeur takes over. Knowing the code, and rules of the mob, Frank, the chauffeur takes on the streets, making them his own. With his strict beliefs, Frank manages to make it to the top, and becomes force to not be reckoned with. He also buys his mom a house." "
